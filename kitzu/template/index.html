Help Center

Buying your first Car

1: Determine your price range
First Choice wants you to make good choices.

We are committed to affordability and we encourage our customers to choose payment plans that fit well with their lifestyle rather than over committing.

2: Identify your likes and dislikes
We want you to find everything you need here.

By determining what features you want or do not want on your car, you can significantly reduce the amount of car options until you find one that suits you best.

3: Find your dream car
Picking a car you love is important.

At First Time our top priority is guaranteeing that you are satisfied with your vehicle, because your first car is usually the first big purchase of your life!

 

4: Make your down payment
The larger the down payment, the lower the monthly cost.

By alleviating yourself of as much of the cost of your car as possible, we can commit to providing you with significantly lower leasing payments.

5: Finance your monthly payments
High credit score, down payments and leasing options determine the monthly cost for your car.

We offer leases from 1-12 months and we try to provide the lowest cost possible for the plan that you choose.

6: Insure your car
We have many trusted insurance companies we recommend to our customers

We recommend some of the highest quality and affordable insurance companies including, Geico, Nationwide, USAA, and Statefarm.
